Transition to Coding

After 3 years at my previous job and many hours of careful consideration, I decided it was time for a change. I found my job to be very unfulfilling and I couldn't realistically picture myself enjoying a long-term career in that industry. Since a kid, I've always loved playing video games - my most fond gaming memories were from games like Runescape, Skyrim and Fallout. A couple years ago I started to involve myself in the cryptocurrency space (and more recently NFT's), and as I delved deeper into the rabbit hole of blockchain technology, I found a true passion for the Web 3.0 industry. Having been surrounded by technology my whole life, I decided to dabble with coding in my spare time and I really enjoyed it. I particularly enjoyed the problem-solving and creativity aspects of coding. So recently I decided to take the leap, quit my job, and commit myself fully to the Coder Academy bootcamp with the aspirations of become a developer.
